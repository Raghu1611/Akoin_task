# Logo Design Choices

The IDR logo icon utilizes a **cube-inspired geometric shape** to represent strong architectural layers, secure structure, and the multifaceted dimensions of risk management. The intersection of layers represents resilience against external threats. 

The color palette features **Orange (#FF6A00)** to symbolize energy, alert readiness, and innovation, contrasted with **Black and White** to establish a high-contrast, professional, and serious undertone appropriate for an educational/advisory institute. 

The typography employs **Inter / Montserrat**, modern sans-serif fonts that inject a clean, minimalist, and forward-looking technology feel. The combination of black and orange typography mirrors the layered contrast of the isometric cube, creating a coherent digital risk identity.
