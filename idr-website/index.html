About IDR

IDR is an industry-led training and deployment institute preparing professionals for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

We believe in building a robust digital risk workforce by driving academic collaboration with UK universities and offering industry-led training. Through real-world risk projects and continuous digital risk workforce development, we bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical execution.
